Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and account credentials, along with the expense data you choose to log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic usage information, such as login timestamps, to help us maintain and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to operate and improve Spendly — this includes displaying your expense history, generating spending summaries, authenticating your account, and providing customer support. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, after which your information will be permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party services for functions such as hosting, analytics, or email delivery. These providers only receive the minimum data necessary to perform their function and are not permitted to use it for any other purpose. Spendly does not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach out to us through the contact details provided within your account settings. We're happy to help clarify anything about how Spendly protects your information.
